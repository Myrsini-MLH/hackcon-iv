What is Hackcon?

Hackcon is the official hackathon organizers' conference. It's an opportunity to meet each other, to learn from each other, and to tackle the issues that are facing together. With two full days of workshops, talks, and discussion sessions, new organizers can learn best practices for running a hackathon, and an veteran organizers will share their experiences and help guide the next generation.

Estes Park, Colorado

Join us in beautiful Estes Park, Colardo for two days of teaching, learning, and community building on a beautiful campground. We'll see you in the mountains!
