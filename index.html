What is Hackcon?

Hackcon is the official hackathon organizers' conference. It's an opportunity for organizers to meet each other, to learn from each other, and to tackle the issues that are faced by hackathon organizers around the world. It's an opportunity for new events to learn best practices for running a hackathon, and an opportunity for veteran organizers to share their experiences and teach the next generation.

Boulder, Colorado

Join us in beautiful Boulder, Colardo for two days of talks, workshops, and discussion sessions as well as outdoor activities where you can meet other members of the hackathon community. We'll see you in the mountains!
